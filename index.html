About the theme

The Observer is a modern & simple theme suitable for almost any type of website and perfect for personal blogs and portfolios.

Observer theme contains most basic layouts such as About, Portfolio, Blog, CV and Contact.

Available in Figma

We published this theme as a free tool to Figma Community, so people can enjoy and integrate it themselves or just have a simple inspiratonal tool in their design process.
